Privacy Policy for Trees Pinyin

Last updated: February 2024

1. Data Collection

Trees Pinyin does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. All processing of Chinese characters and conversion to the PYT system happens locally on your device.

2. Use of Permissions

  • activeTab: Used only to identify and modify Chinese characters on the webpage you are currently viewing to show PYT phonics.
  • storage: Used only to save your preferences (e.g., font size, display settings).

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties. No data is sold or used for advertising purposes.

4. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us through our official support channels provided in the Chrome Web Store.
